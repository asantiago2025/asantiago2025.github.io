Faces of Hallyu Wave

And of course, we can't forget… FOOD!

Most Korean dramas showcase the exquisiteness of Korean cuisine, influencing its viewers to crave Korean food.

Kimchi, ramyeon, bibimbap, tteokbokki, samgyeopsal, kimbap, and bulgogi are some of the most popular Korean dishes in the Philippines. Many Korean restaurants have been established in the country to cater to the increasing Korean food market.

Online videos like the Samyang ramyeon challenge or the spicy noodle challenge have also been circulating on social media.

Restaurants and retail stores have also seized the opportunity to earn more profit, with many selling ramyeon.
