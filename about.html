Hey! How's it going? I'm Jace, the person behind this one-man assemble, The World of Hallyu.

I decided to make a magazine about the Hallyu Fever in the Philippines, its brief history on how it started, and some of its faces or aspects, for I've been a huge enthusiast of almost anything related to the Korean Wave. In fact, I was even assigned to create a presentation and stage a mini 'webinar' about Hallyu in our homeroom session last school year. I just simply love to learn and explore Hallyu — whether it be the ​​Korean pop culture, Korean music, Korean dramas, Korean food, or Korean fashion — to the extent that could even reach the galaxies (just kidding, haha). This webpage is basically a whole world or planet on Hallyu, hence, the name and theme of the magazine.

Have a fun time scrolling! XOXO
