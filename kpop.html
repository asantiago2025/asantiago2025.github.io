Faces of Hallyu Wave

K-pop is loved for its iconic girl and boy groups whose members all go through stringent singing and dance training before debuting. Some notable K-pop stars with large fanbases here in the country other than the aforementioned are Rain, Bigbang, Super Junior, Girls' Generation, Red Velvet, IU, and SHINEE. K-pop groups, after all, have infiltrated world-famous talk shows and award shows, with the support of the South Korean government and highly competent agencies.

With the dawn of highly trained professional entertainers and unmatched performances, K-pop fans began to emerge from around the world and flock to South Korea in hopes of catching a pop concert and seeing their favorite Korean idols in person. The influence of K-pop has also sparked the interest of international fans for concerts, cultural tours, and even merchandise. The remarkable performances of various Korean idol groups attract a lot of Filipinos even if the songs were sung in a different language.

Once doubted as a fad that will soon disappear, the cultural phenomenon that K-pop brought spilled over from music albums to light sticks and other products they endorse.
